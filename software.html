About The Company

LuckyStreak is an Israeli-based developer of live dealer games established as recently as 2014. This privately-held company currently employs 70+ people, among which experts in online behaviour and product development. Live gaming is their specialty, although LuckyStreak also offers RNG games such as slots, fixed-odd games, video poker and card games. Their player-first mentality has earned them quite a few fans within the international gaming community, and subsequently facilitated entry into quite a few reputable online casinos. The company is operating with a Curacao license. Their studios are located in Latvia and Lithuania, which results in multi-lingual dealers usually speaking Russian, English and at least 1 additional language.
